Gold Coast TechSpace Discovery workshops run during the school holidays and on ocassional weekends in term time, they include Arduino Electronics, Robotics, Lego Mindstorms, Minecraft, Programming and lots more.

They are a great way for kids to learn about technology in a relaxed and more casual atmosphere than school. We encourage kids to explore their ideas: creativity and experimentation are encouraged!

Our facilitators are all geeks - exploring and experimenting with technology is a big part of our lives and we would like to encourage this spirit in the next generation. All facilitators hold valid Qld Blue Cards.
